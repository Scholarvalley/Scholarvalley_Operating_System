About Us

Scholarvalley was founded with a vision: Knowledge is power.

The goal of ScholarValley is to help students with admission into Universities in United States of America as well as Canada. Our first priority is to help students reduce the total cost of their education. We guarantee 75% of our applicants partial or full scholarships as well as an admission in at least three universities in the country of their choice (United States or Canada). Full scholarships are mostly based on school performance. Partial scholarships depend on multiple other factors. Our specialty is to acquire them for you.

Scholarvalley is an admission company with its headquarter located in Cincinnati OH, USA. We work with a large group of companies as well as Universities and colleges to guarantee you admission. Our goal is to help you get admission into the University of your choice at a lower cost.

Our previous customers were able to obtain admission into 36 different universities in the United States as well as Canada. We are very successful in achieving our goal and we standby our work.
