Dear students,

Here are the steps Scholarvalley follows:

  • Document review: The document review is a $5 for Prospective Freshman / $10 for Continuing Undergraduate / $15 for Graduate Student allowing us to determine your chances to get some scholarships. From this document review a statistical percentage of chance will be generated.
  • Applications: Once the document review has been done, applications has to be submitted to our universities partners. An Scholarvalley ambassador will guide you with the application process and contacting our partners universities.
  • Scholarship: Upon scholarship receipt, please notify your Scholarvalley officer.

Please see attached the list of documents required in order to do the evaluation. If you have any other questions, please feel free to email us at:

support@scholarvalley.com
